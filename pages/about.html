Animals big and small are welcome

Taking care of animals has become a lifestyle that we enjoy and we are dedicated to it for many coming years.

Been taking care of animals since 97'

For over two decades, our passion for animal welfare has driven everything we do. Since opening our doors in 1997, we’ve been dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals in need — giving them a second chance at life and love. From dogs and cats to small critters and farm animals, every creature in our care is treated with kindness, compassion, and respect. With the help of our devoted team and generous community, we continue to fight for a future where every animal is safe, loved, and valued.


We are a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals in need.
