Every dog needs a home

Adopting dogs allow for new exciting adventures in your life. Take the next step.

Apply for the adoption

Follow these simple steps to find your forever friend.

Step 1: Browse Available Animals

Explore our gallery of adorable cats, dogs, and other animals currently looking for loving homes.

Step 2: Submit an Enquiry Form

Once you find a pet you’re interested in, email us ST10478121@rcconnect.edu.za to let us know more about you and your home.

Step 3: SPCA Contact & Pre-Screening

Our adoption team will review your email and contact you for a short phone or email chat to confirm a few details.

Step 5:: Home Check & Final Approval

For some adoptions, a brief home check may be needed to ensure the space is safe and suitable. Once approved, we’ll finalize the adoption!

Step 6: Bring Home Your New Friend

Pick a date to bring your pet home! We’ll provide starter care tips, vaccination info, and everything else you need for a smooth transition.
